Today's Meal

Ingredients

    - 6 oz steak - 1/2 avocado - Mixed greens - Cherry tomatoes - Olive oil - Balsamic vinegar

Instructions

1. Cook the steak to your desired doneness. 2. Slice the avocado and cherry tomatoes. 3. Toss mixed greens with olive oil and balsamic vinegar. 4. Top salad with sliced steak, avocado, and cherry tomatoes.
